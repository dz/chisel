Colophon

This log is a journal of personal opinion1, an occasional code scrap, and a coffee-table picture book. Generated by chisel, version controlled by git, and served by github, it is a personal site produced for a unit audience (family and a handful of friends) that knows the author and this site’s URL well.

Author

Dad to two lovely kids, python loving bike nut, the author is a 40 years old engineer much into offshore structures and turret-mooring systems. Outside of work, this is his creative outlet.

While this log is written mostly by the author, it is not uncommon to see occasional posts authored by his two children. Such posts would have a byline, and an afterword.
